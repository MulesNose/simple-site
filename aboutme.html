About Me

   I grew up in beautiful, South Central Pennsylvania. I'm currently living in Hoboken, NJ and attending Stevens Institute of Technology. I'm pursuing a BE in Engineering Management and a Minor in Economics. Furthermore, I'm also studying to complete my Masters of Engineering (ME) in Systems Engineering with a concentration in Data Exploration and Visualization for Risk and Decision-Making.

   I played for the Stevens Mens Varsity Volleyball Team for the first two years I attended Stevens. In my spare time I enjoy running, cooking, reading, and writing. Furthermore, I study programming in my spare time to build a foundation for computer networking and data analysis. I am fascinated by data mining and visualization because of the potential these, and many other, tools have for progress in not only the energy industry, but all industries.
